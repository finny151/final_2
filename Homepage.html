Welcome to VT Gear Recommendations!

This site is designed to help you make decisions when it comes to purchasing gear for your upcoming adventures in Vermont. After working in a ski shop for the past four years and coaching mountain biking in the summers, I have accumulated an ever growing index of gear options. You can navigate this site through the toolbar at the top or by clicking on the icons to get to specific Ski and Bike recommendations (with video reviews!). If you are constrained by a budget go check out the "Gear Finder" tab to see what might be best for you. If you have any questions feel free to reach out to me directly, my contact info is at the bottom of your page. Cheers!
